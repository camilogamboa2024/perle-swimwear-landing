# UX Baseline (pre premium)

## Current state
- Visual language exists but with minimal tokenization and repeated inline styles.
- Header/footer are functional but not fully semantic or premium in spacing hierarchy.
- Product home cards are simple and rely on inline image placeholders.
- Product detail is a raw list of variants (no selector UX).
- Cart lacks inline quantity controls and remove actions in UI.
- Checkout has basic inputs and limited error/success hierarchy.
- Legal pages are functional and WhatsApp conditional copy is already safe.

## Mobile pain points
- Navigation wraps but lacks intentional mobile interaction pattern.
- Cart and checkout spacing/typography are utilitarian.
- No skeleton/empty-state visual treatment quality.

## Accessibility notes
- Focus styles exist but not consistently applied across all controls.
- Landmark/aria semantics can be improved in nav/action areas.
